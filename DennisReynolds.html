Dennis Reynolds

Dennis Reynolds is a fictional character on the FX series It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia, portrayed by Glenn Howerton.

He is the co-owner of Paddy's Pub, alongside his childhood friends:

  • Charlie Kelly
  • Mac (roommate)
  • His twin, Dee Dee Reynolds
  • His legal dad, Frank Reynolds

Character Overview

Dennis is the twin brother of Dee and co-owner of Paddy's Pub. He has been friends with Mac and Charlie since childhood. The most amoral member of the group, Dennis is narcissistic, hypersexual, selfish, and abrasive.

He is a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania and has shown to have extensive knowledge of psychology. Dennis has shown to be predatory towards women, as shown in a self-described system where he exploits women's trust and abuses them emotionally in order to have sex with them known as the D.E.N.N.I.S. system.
